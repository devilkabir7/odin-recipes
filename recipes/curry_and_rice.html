Curry and rice

Description

Wild curry and rice. The curry is made with the special roux and sauce, and is perfect for those who don't like too much spice.

Ingredients

  • For chicken
    • Chicken thighs
    • Carrots
    • Onions
    • Potatoes
    • Ginger
    • Garlic
  • For curry
    • Chicken broth
    • Honey
    • Soy sauce
    • Ketchup
    • Roux
    • Salt

Steps

  1. Prep:Cut call the solid ingredients
  2. Mix:Mix all the solid ingredients
  3. Prepare curry:Add oil and add mixed ingredients and roux
  4. Slow cook:Cook slowly and skim foam off the top
  5. Wait:Cook until carrots and poatoes are soft
