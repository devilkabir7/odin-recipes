Ramen

Description

World famous ramen noodle soup.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Broth
  • Dried shittake mushrooms
  • Carrots and kale
  • Egg
  • Chili oil

Steps

  1. Stir fry aromatics:Stir fry garlic and ginger
  2. Make soup:Add broth and shittake mushrooms
  3. Add noodles:Cook noodles along with broth
  4. Add veggies:Add thinly sliced/shredded veggies and cook until tender
  5. Toppings:Add a soft boiled egg and chili oil for topping off
