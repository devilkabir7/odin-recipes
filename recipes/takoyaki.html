Takoyaki

Description

These eight pieces of cheese-covered takoyaki have a flavorful blend of sauce and cheese that's irresistible.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Dashi(Japanese stock soup) powder
  • Soy sauce
  • Octopus/tako
  • Green onions
  • Powdered seaweed
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix:Mix the batter.
  2. Prep:Prep the fillings.
  3. Pour and fill:Pour batter in greased pan.
  4. Filp:Turn takoyaki 90 degrees.
  5. Sauce:When crisp, pop them out and brush with sauce and grate some cheese.
